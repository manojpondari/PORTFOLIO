Project 3

Innovative Anti-Counterfeiting using Blockchain

This project identifies counterfeit products by integrating barcode scanning with blockchain technology. Eachproduct's barcode is linked to a blockchain ledger, ensuring an immutable record of its origin and journey through the supply chain. Consumers and stakeholders can scan barcodestoinstantlyverifyproductauthenticity,reducingfakegoods. The blockchain ledger ensures data integrity with Hashing Algorithms(SHA 256), ConsensusAlgorithms, Smart Contracts by providing a transparent and tamper-proof system. This solution enhances trust and security in supply chains. The proposed system is scalable and adaptable to various industries.
